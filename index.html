About Me

  • AP Spanish Language & Culture

What I'm Up To Right Now

I'm a senior (Class of 2017) at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology, a Governor's STEM school in Alexandria, VA.

My coursework is focused on Computer Science. I'm fascinated by application development and cybersecurity and have taken classes like Mobile Application Development (taught using Android) and Web Application Development. Next year I will be working in our Mobile and Application Development Laboratory for my senior research project.

I'm also passionate about community service, social entrepreneurship, and public speaking- especially when it relates to CS, youth, and STEM education! Please contact me if you are interested in working together!

Everybody Code Now!, Founder & Executive Director

I was able to discover my passion for Computer Science thanks to TJ and an incredible group of mentors that encouraged me along the way. It was important to me that such opportunities existed for everyone from a young age.

That's why I founded Everybody Code Now! and serve as the Executive Director. So far, we have exposed more than 500 students to Computer Science and helped schools raise more than $15,000 to fund future STEM activities in schools.

If you're interested in learning more or working together, please visit our website at everybodycodenow.org.

Ashburn Teen Leadership Council, Director

I've served as the Director of the Ashburn Teen Leadership Council for three years, working with area youth and the Ashburn Library to improve teen engagement and encourage teens to do community service.I was recently re-elected to continue serving through August 2017.
More information about the Council can be found at ashburntlc.weebly.com.
